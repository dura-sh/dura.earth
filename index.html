DuraChain

Decentralized medical equipment.

DuraChain is a decentralized Durable Medical Equipment (DME) software company. We improve patient care by helping providers with streamlining clinical documentation, billing, and reporting needs. Utilizing blockchain technology we can automate processes, have higher success rates, and decrease the time for a patient to receive their DME. Teaming up with some of the best DME companies in the world, we are here to decentralize the software and revolutionize the industry.
